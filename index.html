Core Technology

AI-Powered Recommendation Engine

The key to bringing the right product to the right people is having an advanced and intuitive recommendation engine. It’s simple, we collect health and demographic information, combine it with big-data sources, and accurately recommend the right insurance product for their lifestyle.

Instant Decision Insurance Products

Our first focus when building the Pendella experience was to integrate products that makes both the agent and customers lives easier. The Instant Decision insurance products within our suite require no medical exams and can be purchased & secured in under 10 minutes.

Point-of-Sale & Licensed Agent Assist

Some customers have a more complicated medical history, and will find it difficult to get approvals for Instant Decision products. Our Quote and Apply products allow users to get a quote, begin an application, and schedule with an agent within a few minutes.
